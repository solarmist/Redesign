Welcome to wyldthingz, located 60 minutes north of San Francisco in our own "wyld" corner of Wine Country. Here, we specialize in well rounded, TICA registered Savannah Cats and Savannah Kittens since 2004.

Our litters are limited so that each kitten is treated as part of the family, accustomed to children, other pets, and all the normal activities of the home. This ensures that our savannah kittens are certain to adjust well with any new situation and bond easily with its new family.

Show, Breeding cats, and pets available occasionally. Quality and not Quantity is our goal at Wyldthingz. I plan on focusing on producing SILVER spotted savannahs but we do have all colors and often the destinctive black noses, like the serval.

See our kittens for current kittens available. I update photos often. For the privacy of our cats and our family, visiting is reserved for serious kitten buyers only.

Ozo is a ebony silver F5C male from Allison Navarro of Jewels of the Nile. Fingers are crossed that this silly, sweet young fellow will eventually sire silver SBT kittens at Wyldthingz in the future. Right now he still thinks he's a kitten. He has dark spotting and well rounded ears that should compliment the Wyldthingz girls well.

Shown at the TICA annual in 2010 with 9 other savannah in the ring, he was well liked for his rich color, nice type, and great Savannah Build. He took Best of Breed in one ring as the "Nicest silver I have ever seen" by ring judge Karen McInchak. Thanks Karen! We are proud of him!
